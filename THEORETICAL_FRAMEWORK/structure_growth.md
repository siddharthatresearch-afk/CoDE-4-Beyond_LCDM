# Linear Growth Evolution

$$
\delta'' + \left( \frac{3}{a} + \frac{H'}{H} \right)\delta' - \frac{3 H_0^2 \Omega_m}{2 a^5 H^2}\delta = 0
$$

where:
- $\delta$ represents the linear matter density contrast ($\delta\rho_m/\rho_m$),
- $a$ represents the cosmic scale factor,
- and $H(a)$ is the modified CoDE-4 expansion history.

The altered expansion evolution changes the effective friction term and growth evolution of matter perturbations.
